Clothing for Back to School

by Catie's Closet in Dracut, MA

This back to school season, help support young schoolchildren living in poverty. Oftentimes, these children don't have the resources to obtain approprite apparel for school. Without enough clothes, these children lose confidence, leading to higher rates of absenteeism. Support them today by donating essential clothes for the beginning of school.

Kids with only tattered clothes to wear to school suffer from low self-esteem and become the target of bullying. It’s incredible how age-appropriate clothing can help boost self-esteem and school attendance. With your help this season, Catie’s Closet can serve more Pre-K to 12th Grade kids in low-income neighborhoods.

Every child deserves to fit in

Please consider donating new or lightly used clothing to empower children to learn and connect with their peers, regardless of socioeconomic status. We are looking for clothes for this fall's back to school season. Donations of new sneakers and flip flops, t-shirts, shorts, jeans, hoodies, underwear, socks, and backpacks are especially valuable this time of year, but any and all types of clothes are accepted.
